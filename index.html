💡 Dashboard Lights Reference

Quick reference guide for dashboard warning lights.

Check Engine

Warning

Could indicate minor or serious issues. Get code scanned.

Oil Pressure

Critical

Stop driving immediately. Check oil level.

Coolant Temperature

Critical

Engine overheating. Pull over safely.

Battery

Warning

Charging system issue. Could be alternator or battery.

ABS Light

Info

Anti-lock brakes disabled. Standard brakes work.

Airbag

Warning

Airbag system fault. Get inspected soon.

Tire Pressure (TPMS)

Info

One or more tires low. Check pressure.

Brake

Critical

Parking brake on or brake fluid low. Check immediately.

Transmission

Warning

Transmission issue. Pull over safely.
